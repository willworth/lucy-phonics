# Recording Script — Phase 1

**Equipment:** Zoom H6, quiet room
**Format:** WAV, 44.1kHz, mono
**Tips:** Keep mouth ~15cm from mic. Pause 1 second between each clip. Martha will trim, normalise, and convert.

**CRITICAL:** No schwa on consonants! "mmm" not "muh", "sss" not "suh", "t-" not "tuh".

---

## 1. Isolated Phonemes (6 clips)

Say each sound cleanly, hold for about 1 second:

1. **mmm** (continuous hum, like you're thinking)
2. **sss** (hissing snake, continuous)
3. **aaa** (short 'a' as in apple, not 'ay')
4. **t-** (sharp tap, tongue behind teeth — very short)
5. **p-** (pop of air from lips — very short)
6. **nnn** (continuous hum through nose)

---

**Total: 50 clips. Estimated recording time: 15-20 minutes.**

After recording, transfer the WAV files to the server (email to martha@agentmail.to, scp, or USB). Martha will:
1. Split into individual clips (if recorded as one long take)
2. Trim silence
3. Normalise to -16 LUFS
4. Convert to MP3 128kbps mono
5. Build Howler.js sprite sheet
